# Trademark Usage

## QUICK REFERENCE

  

### Do's

  * Use the Pulumi logo to link to Pulumi
  * Use the Pulumi logo in social buttons to link to your Pulumi profile or project
  * Use the Pulumi logo to advertise that your product has built-in Pulumi integration
  * Use the Pulumi logo in a blog post or news article about Pulumi

  

### Dont's

  * Use the Pulumi logo for your application's icon
  * Create a modified version of the Pulumi logo
  * Integrate the Pulumi logo into your logo
  * Use any Pulumi artwork without permission
  * Sell any Pulumi artwork without permission
  * Change the colors, dimensions or add your own text/images

  

### Please contact us

  * If you want to use artwork not included in this repository
  * If you want to use these images in a video/mainstream media

  

### Naming projects and products

Please avoid naming your projects anything that implies Pulumi's endorsement.
This also applies to domain names.
